Tablentor is a WordPress plugin that extends Elementor with two powerful table widgets: **Basic Table Builder** for manually crafting structured tables, and **CSV Table** for importing data directly from CSV files. Both widgets offer rich styling controls that integrate seamlessly with Elementor's design system.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the plugin">
    Search for **Tablentor** in **Plugins → Add New** in your WordPress admin, then click **Install Now** and **Activate**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Elementor">
    Edit any page or post with Elementor. Search for **Basic Table** or **Table CSV** in the widget panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a widget">
    Drag and drop a Tablentor widget onto your canvas. Configure columns, add content or CSV data, and adjust styling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Click **Publish** or **Update** in Elementor to make your table live on the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Tablentor requires **Elementor** (free version) to be installed and active. It is compatible with both Elementor Free and Elementor Pro.
</Note>
