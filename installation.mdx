---
title: "Installation"
description: "Install and activate Tablentor in your WordPress site."
---

Tablentor can be installed directly from the WordPress plugin directory or manually by uploading a zip file.

## Automatic installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard">
    Navigate to your site's admin area (typically `yoursite.com/wp-admin`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Plugins > Add New">
    In the left sidebar, click **Plugins**, then **Add New**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for Tablentor">
    Type `Tablentor` in the search field at the top right of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the plugin">
    Locate the Tablentor plugin in the results and click **Install Now**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate the plugin">
    Once installation is complete, click **Activate** to enable Tablentor on your site.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manual installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the plugin zip file">
    Download the Tablentor `.zip` archive from the WordPress plugin directory or your distribution source.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in to your WordPress admin dashboard">
    Navigate to your site's admin area (typically `yoursite.com/wp-admin`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin">
    In the left sidebar, click **Plugins > Add New**, then click the **Upload Plugin** button at the top of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload the zip file">
    Click **Choose File**, select the downloaded `.zip` archive, then click **Install Now**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate the plugin">
    After installation completes, click **Activate Plugin** to enable Tablentor on your site.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After activation

Once Tablentor is active, follow these steps to use the widgets in Elementor:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a page in Elementor">
    Edit any existing page or post by clicking **Edit with Elementor**, or create a new page and open it in the Elementor editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Tablentor widgets">
    In the left widget panel, search for `Basic Table` or `Table CSV`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag and drop a widget">
    Drag your chosen widget from the panel onto the canvas at the desired location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure table settings and content">
    Use the **Content** tab to set column count, add rows, paste CSV data, and configure display options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish the page">
    Click **Publish** (or **Update** for an existing page) to make the table live on your site.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Tablentor widgets appear under the **Basic** category in the Elementor widget panel.
</Tip>
